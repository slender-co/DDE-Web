Our Mission

A Promise to Rebuild

The devastating fires that swept through Altadena left scars on the land and in the hearts of our community. But where others see destruction, we see opportunity—an opportunity to rebuild stronger, smarter, and more resilient than before.

Dirt Dudes Excavating has opened a dedicated office and operations center in Altadena, CA, with a singular focus: to restore the land, rehabilitate the terrain, and help our neighbors rebuild their homes and their lives.

This isn't just another project for us. It's a commitment. A promise. A responsibility we take to heart. We've been serving California communities for over 40 years, and now we're bringing that expertise, dedication, and care directly to Altadena.

Our Services

Complete Rebuilding Services

We bring the full spectrum of our capabilities to Altadena—the same expertise, equipment, and services that have served California for over 40 years, now dedicated to fire recovery and rebuilding.

From initial site preparation through final inspections, we handle every phase of your rebuild project with precision, professionalism, and care.

Grading & Pad Development

Complete site grading and pad preparation for rebuilding. We handle everything from debris removal to final grade, ensuring your property is ready for construction.

  • Site clearing and debris removal
  • Precise pad grading and compaction
  • Erosion control and soil stabilization
  • Drainage and water management systems

Concrete & Foundations

Complete concrete services for rebuilding: foundations, slabs, driveways, patios, and decorative finishes. All slab types, stamped, colored, and custom designs.

  • Foundation footings and grade beams
  • All slab types (monolithic, post-tensioned, suspended)
  • Stamped, colored, and decorative concrete
  • Driveways, walkways, and outdoor spaces

Utility Installation

Complete utility infrastructure installation and restoration. We handle all underground utilities required for rebuilding.

  • Water and sewer line installation
  • Electrical and gas utility connections
  • Storm drain and drainage systems
  • Utility trenching and backfilling

Project Management

Comprehensive project management from planning through completion. We coordinate all phases, manage timelines, and ensure seamless execution.

  • Project planning and scheduling
  • Daily progress tracking and updates
  • Subcontractor coordination
  • Quality control and inspections

Permitting & Compliance

We handle all permitting, approvals, and regulatory compliance. Our deep knowledge of local codes and processes streamlines your rebuild.

  • Building permit applications and processing
  • Grading and excavation permits
  • Utility connection approvals
  • Code compliance and inspections

Land Restoration

Complete land rehabilitation and restoration services to rebuild the natural foundation of fire-affected properties.

  • Soil stabilization and remediation
  • Erosion control and slope stabilization
  • Vegetation restoration planning
  • Final site cleanup and preparation
